Picture Perfect Trip!

Where would you go?

I would travel to Japan, specifically visiting the hectic city of Tokyo and Shibuya. Tokyo boasts giant skyscrapers, diverse culture, and magnificent temples, where as Shibuya is renowned for its lively pedestrian crosswalk and fashion streets.

Tokyo city skyline

What would you do?

In Tokyo, I would tour historic sites like the Senso-ji Temple, shop in Harajuku, and party in Shibuya. I would also attend teamLab Planets, a great digital art museum. In Shibuya, I'd watch the famous Scramble Crossing and shop in trendy malls, and all the anime shops and view the different tech that have not yet been viewed in the united states.

Shibuya Crossing

What food would you eat?

I would eat delicious Japanese food like sushi, ramen, takoyaki (octopus balls), and matcha sweets. I would try street food at Tsukiji Outer Market and eat a meal in a traditional sushi restaurant.
